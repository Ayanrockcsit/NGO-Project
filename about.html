Who we are ?

We believe that collective efforts can bring about lasting change, and we work tirelessly to create opportunities for those in need.NJO's Impact Reports highlight the tangible and measurable impact of our programs and are published in October every year while Seva's Annual Reports cover everything in the Impact Reports but also include an overview of Seva's finances and are published in December every year.

OUR VISION

We envision a world where compassion drives action, and every individual has the opportunity to thrive. By fostering social change through various programs and partnerships, we strive to build a more equitable and sustainable future for all

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to uplift marginalized communities by providing essential resources, education, and healthcare support. We aim to create a society where everyone has access to basic necessities and the opportunity to lead a dignified life.

What We Do

Education: Providing scholarships, school supplies, and learning resources to underprivileged children. Healthcare: Organizing free medical camps and providing essential healthcare services to those in need. Women Empowerment: Supporting women with skill development programs, financial assistance, and self-sustainability projects. Environmental Conservation: Promoting sustainability through tree plantation drives, waste management initiatives, and awareness campaigns.
